3) How to apply a click event to all paragraphs except for the last?
A) We can apply the .not() selector with :last to filter the last element:

    $('#elementName li').not(':last').on('click', someFunction);

4) Why does the alert show "Jack" first and then undefined?
A) Since there is no semicolon placed after the return statement, JavaScript auto adds semicolons and therefore an undefined is returned after Jack is returned by the alert.
